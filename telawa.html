Mahmoud Khalil Al-Hosary

Sheikh Mahmoud Khalil Al-Hosary (Arabic: ٱلشِـيـْخ محمُود خَلِيـْل الْحُصـري‎), also known as Al-Hosari, was an Egyptian Qāri who was widely known for his accurate reading of the Quran.
"Alama Al Quran" as well as all the considered Arab Qur'an tutors of the Holy Quran recommend you to listen more to Sheikh Al Hosari to improve your Qur'an recitation of the Holy Quran.
